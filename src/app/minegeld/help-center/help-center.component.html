Homepage

On the homepage, the feeling of sophistication, prosperity, balance and stability that the site
conveys to the reader is remarkable.

In the Homepage, the CHECK OUR COURSES option directs the visitor to the courses tab
where you will see a variety of courses, which will help and guide our customers through this
journey of success and solution about their financial life.

Also in Homepage, we have a brand to install our Mobile App. Then you check out QR CODE to
download our app "PatoDin", which is a personal finance organization and education
application, developed for children and teenagers to start organizing their finances and money.

And finally, you have the option to install PatoDin in some devices. PatoDin is now available in
Android, IOS, Macbook, Windows, Linux, and more. When you click in "Download Now", it
redirects you into a installation page, where you can find your specifically device, and install in
your device.
